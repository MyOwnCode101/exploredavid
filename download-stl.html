Model Overview

The original 3D model of Michelangelo’s David was created by Hugo Navarro (@hugonvrro_2062955) and published on Printables.com. ExploreDavid adapted and optimized the file specifically for educational desktop 3D printing, including slicing, scaling, and structural segmentation.

Credit: “David” by Hugo Navarro | Source: Printables.com

Print Recommendations

  • Printer: FDM (e.g., Bambu Lab A1 Mini)
  • Material: PLA or PLA+
  • Layer Height: 0.08 – 0.12 mm
  • Supports: Enabled
  • Adhesion: Brim or raft advised

Finishing Guidelines

  • Use epoxy putty or filler for seam coverage
  • Sand with 800+ grit paper for surface refinement
  • Optionally apply a matte primer to mimic marble
