Model Overview

The original 3D scan of David was produced by Scan the World, an open cultural heritage initiative by MyMiniFactory. ExploreDavid adapted and optimized the file specifically for desktop 3D printing, including slicing, scaling, and structural segmentation.

Credit: “David” by Scan the World | Scan the World – MyMiniFactory | Licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0

Print Recommendations

  • Printer: FDM (e.g., Bambu Lab A1 Mini)
  • Material: PLA or PLA+
  • Layer Height: 0.08 – 0.12 mm
  • Supports: Enabled
  • Adhesion: Brim or raft advised

Finishing Guidelines

  • Use epoxy putty or filler for seam coverage
  • Sand with 800+ grit paper for surface refinement
  • Optionally apply a matte primer to mimic marble
