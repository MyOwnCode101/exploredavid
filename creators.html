Meet the Creators

This project is the result of collaboration between two students combining digital development, design, and 3D printing. Together, we aimed to bring Michelangelo's David into the digital world while preserving its artistic and cultural integrity.

Lucio Valerio Carucci

GitHub LinkedIn Instagram Website

Technical Director & Lead Developer

Lucio was responsible for the full digital architecture of the project. He designed and coded the interactive website, implemented the integration of the 3D model via Sketchfab, and oversaw the full deployment of ExploreDavid.com. He also wrote and structured all the textual content, managed licensing compliance, optimized performance for multi-device compatibility, and coordinated visual consistency across pages. Lucio conducted thorough testing and refinement to ensure technical and aesthetic quality throughout the experience.

Daniele Pio Pica

X TikTok Instagram Website

3D Designer & Visual Artist

Responsible for preparing, optimizing, and printing the 3D model using Bambu Lab tools. They also created the project’s official logo and contributed to the artistic direction of the presentation, ensuring the blend of tradition and technology was clear and compelling.
