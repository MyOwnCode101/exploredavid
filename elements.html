Meet the Creators

This project is the result of collaboration between two students combining digital development, design, and 3D printing. Together, we aimed to bring Michelangelo's David into the digital world while preserving its artistic and cultural integrity.

Lucio Valerio Carucci

Technical Director & Lead Developer

Lucio designed and coded the interactive website, integrated the 3D model using advanced tools like Three.js and model-viewer, and managed the full deployment of exploredavid.com. He also handled the licensing, descriptions, and visual presentation of the project.
